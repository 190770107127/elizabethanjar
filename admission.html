ADMISSION PROCEDURE

__ _



Principal message

For - L.K.G. To Std-9

__ _



Admissions for the classes will be according to the vacancy available. Vacancy in a particular class is possible only if any student discontinues his/her studies and take the Leaving Certificate.

Information regarding the actual number of vacancies in a particular class will be available only after results are declared or first week of May

For -Std-11 Comm.

__ _



Our school is affiliated with Gujarat Secondary & Higher Secondary Education Board, Gandhinagar. The Admission Forms for Std. 11th Science & Commerce are available on the same day of the declaration of SSC Board Exam Results of Gujarat Board, that is generally in the end of May or beginning of June.

All admissions are given on merit basis. The students from other boards such as CBSE and ICSE can also apply.
